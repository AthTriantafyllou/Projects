Crete in Six Days

Six days is enough to fall in love with a place — if you're in the right hands. This trip takes you from the Venetian harbour of Chania's old town to the depths of the Cretan Sea, up into the White Mountains and back again. Every day has a purpose. Every night has a story.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival & Welcome BBQ

Arrive at Chania Airport and make your way to the accommodation. At 18:00 we fire up the BBQ — meet the group, hear the plan, and settle in over food and good company. Later, those who fancy it can head into Chania city for the evening (return independently).

Day 2

Diving Experience I

09:00 – 14:00: First guided scuba session in the Cretan Sea. Crystal-clear water, sea life, and underwater rock formations — all levels welcome, full equipment included. Post-dive lunch back at base, then a free afternoon to rest or explore.

Day 3

Diving Experience II — or Surf

09:00 – 14:00: Second dive session, going a little deeper. Lunch, then beach time and relaxation. In the evening an optional trip into Chania city or dinner out (at your own cost).
Prefer not to dive? Join a surf session instead — no extra cost, weather permitting. Want more water time? Additional dives or a certification course are available as an upgrade.

Day 4

Rest & Explore Chania

A free day — no agenda. Sleep in, wander the old town, find a beach, or simply do nothing. This is the day the trip becomes yours.

Day 5

Samaria Gorge & Loutro Village

09:00 – 15:00: Guided hike through the legendary Samaria Gorge, one of Europe's longest. We emerge at the coast and visit the car-free village of Loutro for lunch by the sea before heading back to Chania for our farewell dinner together.

Day 6

Departure

Transfers to Chania Airport. Safe travels — until the next one.
