How it started

A group of friends
with bad cases of wanderlust.

Pure Life started as a group chat. A few friends, a shared obsession with the outdoors, and a growing list of places we wanted to go — but no one to go with. So we started organising it ourselves. We booked the dives, found the trails, picked the tables. And something clicked.

What we were doing wasn't just travel. It was something more intentional. Trips built around experiences, not itineraries. Around people, not packages. Every trip we took left us more certain that this was worth doing properly — and sharing with more people who felt the same pull.

Pure Life is that idea, grown up. We plan everything so you don't have to. Small groups, hand-picked destinations, real adventures. You show up. We handle the rest.
