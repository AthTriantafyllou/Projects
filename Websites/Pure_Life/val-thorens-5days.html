Val Thorens in Five Days

Val Thorens sits at 2300m — the highest ski resort in Europe and part of Les Trois Vallées, the largest linked ski area in the world. We fly into Geneva, transfer up into the Alps, and spend four days on some of the best terrain on the continent. Ski passes, accommodation, transfers, two proper mountain dinners, and the right people to share it all with. You bring your boots — we handle everything else.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival & Welcome Dinner

Fly into Geneva (GVA) — Lyon is an alternative option. We meet at the airport and transfer together up into the Alps by group bus. Check in, get your bearings, and join us in the evening for a proper welcome dinner: raclette, fondue, good wine, and the people you'll be skiing with for the next few days.

Arrival Land at Geneva GVA (or Lyon) — group transfer departs
~3–4 hrs Bus transfer to Val Thorens (2300m) with Alpine Fleet
20:00 Welcome dinner — raclette or fondue at a local restaurant

Day 2

First Ski Day

First full day on the mountain. Clip in and find your legs across the Les Trois Vallées area. Lunch on the slopes if you like, and a classic après ski session as the light changes. Dinner out in the resort in the evening.

08:30 Breakfast & coffee at accommodation
09:30 On the slopes — ski day begins
12:30 Lunch break at slope-side chalet (optional)
16:30 Après ski at the chosen chalet (optional)
19:30 Dinner at a local restaurant in the resort
22:00 Night out (optional)

Day 3

Second Ski Day

Another full day on the mountain. Push further into the valley, find new runs, or revisit the ones that got you. Dinner back at the accommodation tonight — a more relaxed evening around the table.

08:30 Breakfast & coffee at accommodation
09:30 On the slopes — ski day begins
12:30 Lunch break at slope-side chalet (optional)
16:30 Après ski (optional)
19:30 Group dinner at the accommodation
22:00 Night out (optional)

Day 4

Third Ski Day — or Spa Day

Your call. A third day on the piste if your legs are willing, or a slower day — spa, a long lunch in the village, a walk through the resort. Val Thorens has everything. The evening is yours.

Day 5

Return to Geneva & Flight Home

Morning check-out and group transfer back to Geneva airport. You leave with a full camera roll, tired legs, and already planning the next one.
