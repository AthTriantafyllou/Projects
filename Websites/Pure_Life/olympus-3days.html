3 Days on Olympus

We start in Thessaloniki, drive to the Prionia trailhead, and spend three days moving through one of the most dramatic landscapes in Europe. Night one is at Oropedio ton Mouseon — the Plateau of the Muses, at 2680m. Night two is at Petrostrouga, after the summit push. We come down through Vathres, where cold mountain pools make the perfect finish. Start point and end point: Thessaloniki.

Day by Day

Day 1

Thessaloniki → Prionia → Oropedio ton Mouseon

We leave Thessaloniki early and drive to the Prionia trailhead at the base of Olympus. From there we hike up through dense forest and open ridge to the Plateau of the Muses — one of the most striking spots on the mountain. The evening is yours at the refuge: rest, prepare, and take in the altitude.

08:00 Departure from Thessaloniki by car
10:30 Arrival at Prionia trailhead (1100m) — gear check
11:00 Start hike up toward Oropedio ton Mouseon (Plateau of the Muses)
15:00 Arrive at Oropedio refuge (2680m) — check in, rest
19:00 Dinner at the refuge — brief on tomorrow's summit push

Day 2

Mytikas Summit Push → Petrostrouga

The big day. An alpine start to reach Mytikas — the highest point in Greece at 2918m — before the afternoon weather builds. The scramble to the summit is technical in places; helmets on, guide leading. Once you're there, the views stretch to the sea. We descend a different route to Petrostrouga for the second night.

05:30 Wake up — light breakfast at the refuge
06:00 Depart for Mytikas via Kakoskala ridge scramble
~10:30 Mytikas summit (2918m) — weather permitting
13:00 Begin descent toward Petrostrouga refuge
15:30 Arrive at Petrostrouga (2200m) — rest and dinner

Day 3

Hike Down → Vathres → Thessaloniki

The final morning is a gradual descent through open terrain and old-growth forest. We stop at Vathres — a series of natural mountain pools fed by snowmelt — before completing the descent to Prionia. From there, the car takes you back to Thessaloniki.

07:00 Morning hike begins — descent from Petrostrouga
10:30 Vathres natural mountain pools — swim stop
13:00 Continue descent to Prionia trailhead
14:00 Car transfer departs for Thessaloniki
~16:30 Arrive back in Thessaloniki
