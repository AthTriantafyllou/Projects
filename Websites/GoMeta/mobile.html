What are “Mobile Apps”
Today

Mobile apps today are more than standalone tools on a phone. They are products that support daily workflows, connect users with services, and create direct relationships between businesses and customers. A well-built app must be easy to use, fast, secure, and designed around real user needs — not just technical features.
